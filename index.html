About The Ranch

Nestled in California’s countryside, our ranch is a destination where luxury, nature, and cannabis culture blend seamlessly. Every experience is crafted to slow time and reconnect you with the land.

Built on values of sustainability, authenticity, and community, we cultivate sun-grown cannabis, support local ecosystems, and create meaningful moments for every guest who visits.

  • Sustainability & responsible cultivation
  • Community-driven experiences
  • Respect for land & heritage
