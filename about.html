About Robinson Creek Ranch

A 500-acre sanctuary in the heart of Northern California

Our Story

Nestled along the pristine waters of Robinson Creek, our ranch has been a quiet steward of Northern California’s land for generations. What began as a traditional working ranch has evolved into a sanctuary where nature, craftsmanship, and culture come together.

Today, Robinson Creek Ranch welcomes guests seeking more than a stay. We offer an authentic connection to the land—where luxury is intentional, sustainability is honored, and every experience is rooted in respect for nature and community.

From sun-grown cannabis cultivation to thoughtfully designed lodging, our story continues through the people who visit, explore, and return.
